# Somewhere Between Timelines — implementation plan

## Guardrails
- Keep the repository private and local until Quin explicitly approves a specific publish action.
- Do not commit real photos, messages, voice notes, or precise location details without review.
- Use labeled placeholders while assets and final copy are pending.
- Use one entrance animation, one ambient effect, and one meaningful interaction per chapter.
- Build mobile-first; verify keyboard access, reduced motion, contrast, and larger screens.

## Seven-chapter build sequence
1. **Signal Found** — timeline scan from 05.07 to the 07.22 birthday signal.
2. **The Website That Brought Me to You** — archived-browser origin story.
3. **05.07: The First Timeline** — five to seven film-strip memories.
4. **The Archive of Ordinary Things** — eight to twelve reviewed, tappable garden objects.
5. **Two Lives at Blue Hour** — California and Pakistan under one moon, without precise locations.
6. **Mission Log: Us** — distance, private humor, recovery, and grounded promises.
7. **07.22: The Birthday Timeline** — promises, readable letter, opt-in voice note, and finale.
